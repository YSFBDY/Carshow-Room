Feature Description
Powerful Engine The Aston Martin DBS Superleggera is equipped with a potent twin-turbocharged V12 engine, delivering impressive horsepower and torque, making it one of the most powerful cars in Aston Martin's lineup.
Sleek Design With its aerodynamic styling and elegant lines, the DBS Superleggera exudes sophistication and athleticism, showcasing Aston Martin's commitment to design excellence and performance.
Enhanced Performance Engineered for exhilarating performance, the DBS Superleggera offers blistering acceleration, precise handling, and responsive steering, delivering an engaging driving experience whether cruising on the highway or attacking corners on the track.
Luxurious Interior Inside the cabin, you'll find a luxurious and refined environment, with handcrafted materials, advanced technology, and exquisite attention to detail, providing comfort and convenience for both driver and passengers.
Transmission Options Equipped with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission, the DBS Superleggera offers seamless gear changes and adaptive performance modes, allowing drivers to tailor the driving experience to their preferences and driving conditions.
Customization Aston Martin offers a range of customization options for the DBS Superleggera, allowing buyers to personalize their car with unique paint colors, interior trims, and optional features, creating a bespoke vehicle that reflects their individual style and taste.
Track Performance While designed for grand touring, the DBS Superleggera is also capable on the track, with advanced performance technologies and aerodynamics that enable high-speed stability and cornering agility, making it a versatile supercar for driving enthusiasts.
