Feature Description
Powerful Engine The Lamborghini Huracán STO is powered by a high-performance naturally aspirated V10 engine, delivering exhilarating horsepower and torque for an adrenaline-fueled driving experience.
Racing-Inspired Design With its aggressive aerodynamics and lightweight construction, the Huracán STO draws inspiration from Lamborghini's motorsport heritage, featuring a distinctive exterior with enhanced downforce and track-focused elements for maximum performance.
Track-Ready Performance Engineered for the track, the Huracán STO offers blistering acceleration, precise handling, and responsive steering, thanks to advanced technologies and race-derived components that ensure exceptional performance on both road and track.
Driver-Centric Interior Inside the cockpit, you'll find a driver-focused environment with racing-inspired seats, carbon fiber accents, and advanced technology, providing a luxurious yet purposeful space that enhances the driving experience and puts the driver in full control.
Transmission Options Equipped with a lightning-fast seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, the Huracán STO delivers seamless gear changes and adaptive performance modes, allowing drivers to extract maximum performance in any driving situation.
Customization Lamborghini offers extensive customization options for the Huracán STO, allowing buyers to personalize their car with bespoke paint colors, interior trims, and performance packages, creating a unique and personalized supercar that reflects their individual style and preferences.
Track Performance Designed for track dominance, the Huracán STO features advanced aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and track-focused suspension tuning, enabling high-speed stability, cornering precision, and braking performance that elevate it to the top of its class on the racetrack.
